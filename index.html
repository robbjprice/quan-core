About

About the Lab

The QUAN-CORE Lab operates within the Centre for Health Informatics, a multidisciplinary research environment designed to transform health data into meaningful evidence through analytics, linkage, and applied research.

The lab focuses on improving data validity, developing robust methodologies, and applying health data to generate insights that improve health systems, clinical decision-making, and population health outcomes.

Hude Quan

Dr. Hude Quan played a foundational role in shaping health informatics at the University of Calgary and helped establish the Centre for Health Informatics as a collaborative hub for data science, research innovation, and interdisciplinary partnership.

His work in administrative data validation, comorbidity measurement, and population health research has had a lasting international impact.

Hude Quan

Dr. Hude Quan was a founding leader in the Centre for Health Informatics and helped establish its vision as a collaborative, data-driven research hub focused on precision health and population health outcomes.

His work advanced the use of administrative data, supported the development of health data standards, and contributed to building a global network of researchers in health informatics.
