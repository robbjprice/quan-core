About the Lab

The QUAN-CORE Lab operates within the Centre for Health Informatics, a multidisciplinary research environment designed to transform health data into meaningful evidence through analytics, linkage, and applied research. ([ijpds.org](https://ijpds.org/article/view/2368?utm_source=chatgpt.com))

The lab focuses on improving data validity, developing robust methodologies, and applying health data to generate insights that improve health systems, clinical decision-making, and population health outcomes.
